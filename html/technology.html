Technology

Artificial intelligence

How does it work?

  • Artificial intelligence is a series of algorithms based on the human brain, that enable a computer to learn broad skills from experience and repetition.

  • Neuron processes images of recyclable materials, digitizing pixels into data, and classifying this data into hundreds of categories by material type, color, form, and other characteristics.

  • Neuron uses machine learning (ML) to continuously improve its ability to recognize materials.

  • As our fleet of automation continuously grows across the globe, our AI exponentially increases its ability to learn from data collected from different locations, growing smarter and more effective every day.

Intelligence at scale

  1. 1 / 3

    Our AI platform, AMP Neuron™, uses computer vision to recognize patterns of specific recyclable materials within a complex waste stream of smashed, folded, and tattered objects — all combined together.

  2. 2 / 3

    Neuron distinguishes different plastic polymers, types of paper, metal containers, and multilayered packages to characterize what's in the recycling stream and what needs to be sorted during different process stages.

  3. 3 / 3

    Neuron then guides robots to sort and recover what material to recycle or contamination to be removed.

Robotic sorting

Sort twice as fast, with unmatched precision

AMP Cortex™ is a high-speed robotic sorting system guided by AMP's groundbreaking artificial intelligence (AI) technology. Our robots intelligently perform physical tasks of sorting, picking, and placing material to achieve up to 99% accuracy and up to 80 picks per minute.

Benefits

  • Labor

    Reduce time and money spent on high-turnover positions

  • Safety

    Increase facility safety and reduce training overhead

  • Material recovery

    Capture more high-value material and boost your bottom line

  • Contamination

    Decrease bale contamination and improve quality

Material Characterisation

Know your materials, drive their recovery

AMP Clarity™ is a web-based data portal that provides real-time material characterization and performance measurement throughout key process stages of your sorting operations. Understand your material flow from inbound processing to bale QC to the end of the line. Command your AMP Cortex™ fleet and optimize your sorting performance in real-time. See with AMP Clarity where you can increase productivity, improve material capture, and take action to gain operational efficiency.
